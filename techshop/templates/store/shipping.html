Shipping Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Shipping Overview

We ship products to locations within Bangladesh and internationally. Our goal is to deliver your orders quickly and safely.

2. Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days after payment confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day.

3. Shipping Methods

We offer the following shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days - BDT 99
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days - BDT 199
  • Free Shipping: Available on orders over BDT 5,000

4. Free Shipping Eligibility

Orders totaling BDT 5,000 or more qualify for free standard shipping. This offer is valid for delivery within Bangladesh only.

5. International Shipping

For international orders, shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Please contact us for specific shipping quotes to your country.

6. Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can track your order status on our website or the shipping carrier's website.

7. Delivery Address

Please ensure your delivery address is correct and complete. We are not responsible for orders delivered to incorrect addresses due to customer error.

8. Shipping Delays

While we strive to deliver on time, shipping delays may occur due to:

  • Weather conditions
  • Holiday periods
  • Customs clearance (international)
  • Shipping carrier issues

9. Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is lost or damaged during shipping, please contact us within 48 hours of the expected delivery date. We will work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue.

10. Contact Information

For shipping inquiries, please contact us at support@techshop.com
